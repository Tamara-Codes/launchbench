# Product roadmap

## Before public launch: marketing SEO and AEO

- Public landing, features, pricing, and FAQ pages with answer-first copy.
- Page metadata, canonicals, Open Graph images, sitemap, and robots policy.
- Keep `/app`, `/login`, `/onboarding`, private media, and API routes out of
  search indexing.
- Organization and SoftwareApplication JSON-LD; FAQ schema only when it
  mirrors visible FAQ content.
- Search Console, Bing Webmaster Tools, analytics, and AI-referral monitoring.
- Content strategy based on the final domain, audience, positioning, and real
  proof—not fabricated claims, reviews, or testimonials.

## Deferred commercial work

- Stripe subscriptions, entitlement checks, invoices, and customer billing UI.
